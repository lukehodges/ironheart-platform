03 Data Flow: Booking Lifecycle

End-to-end sequence from initial customer interaction through completion, payment, and review.

sequenceDiagram
  actor Customer
  participant Portal
  participant Scheduling
  participant Team
  participant CalSync as Calendar-Sync
  participant Booking
  participant Notif as Notification
  participant Workflow
  actor Admin
  actor Staff
  participant Payment
  participant Review
  participant Analytics

  Customer->>Portal: Select service
  Portal->>Scheduling: Check availability
  Scheduling->>Team: Get available staff
  Scheduling->>CalSync: Check external calendars
  Scheduling-->>Portal: Available slots
  Customer->>Portal: Choose slot + confirm
  Portal->>Booking: Create job (RESERVED)
  Booking->>Notif: Send confirmation email
  Booking->>CalSync: Push to Google Calendar
  Booking->>Workflow: Trigger automation
  Admin->>Booking: Confirm (CONFIRMED)
  Booking->>Notif: Send confirmed email
  Staff->>Booking: Complete (COMPLETED)
  Booking->>Payment: Generate invoice
  Booking->>Review: Send review request
  Booking->>Analytics: Update metrics

04 Data Flow: Document Processing (Accountant Use Case)

Demonstrates platform flexibility beyond bookings: an accountancy firm processing client documents through the job lifecycle.

sequenceDiagram
  actor Client
  participant Portal
  participant Jobs
  participant Workflow
  participant Scheduling
  participant Team
  participant Notif as Notification
  actor Accountant
  participant Forms
  actor Manager
  participant Payment

  Client->>Portal: Upload documents
  Portal->>Jobs: Create job (RECEIVED)
  Jobs->>Workflow: Trigger assignment workflow
  Workflow->>Scheduling: Find available accountant
  Scheduling->>Team: Check capacity + skills
  Scheduling-->>Workflow: Assign accountant
  Jobs->>Notif: Send documents received email
  Accountant->>Jobs: Start processing (IN_PROGRESS)
  Accountant->>Forms: Fill compliance checklist
  Accountant->>Jobs: Complete (REVIEW)
  Manager->>Jobs: Approve (COMPLETED)
  Jobs->>Notif: Send ready for collection email
  Jobs->>Payment: Generate invoice
  Client->>Portal: Download deliverables
